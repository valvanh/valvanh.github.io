Let's talk about me

Full-stack web developer specializing in front-end, I am passionate about creating complex projects with simple and efficient tools. After starting in web integration, then exploring full-stack development, I naturally moved towards the front-end, a field where I fully flourish by transforming ideas into interactive and intuitive experiences.

Versatile and curious, I have had the opportunity to experiment with a wide range of technologies and frameworks such as ReactJS, VueJS, AstroJS, Laravel, Symfony, and many others. These explorations have allowed me to develop a complete vision of modern web ecosystems while improving my skills in creating user interfaces that are both efficient and aesthetic.

Today, I am looking for ambitious and innovative projects, whether technically complex or visually captivating. I am particularly attracted to creative coding, which allows me to push the boundaries of web development to design immersive and visually stunning experiences.

My goal: to combine technical expertise and creativity to transform ideas into concrete solutions that leave a lasting impression.
